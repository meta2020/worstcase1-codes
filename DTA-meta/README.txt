Data and codes used in Sections 4.1 (tropnin data) and 4.2 (IVD data)

- anadata/: analytical data created from R

- example data/: data of examples in Section 4.1 and 4.2

- fig-tab/: outputs of figures and tables

- rfunc/: R functions used in the analysis

- SAS/: SAS codes and outputs of optimization

- Analysis-IVD.R: R codes of generating results in Section 4.2

- Analysis-trop.R: R codes of generating results in Section 4.1
